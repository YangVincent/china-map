Three Gorges: what the water levels mean
Schematic longitudinal profile, vertical exaggeration ~1,600×. Shaded area = water added above the natural river. The pool is deliberately run in reverse of the natural cycle: drawn down to 145 m before summer to catch floods, refilled to 175 m in autumn. For the map-view footprint, toggle Reservoir extent: blue = the 145 m pool, orange = the extra land drowned at 175 m (computed from pre-dam SRTM elevation data).
